# Demo Video Script — 3 minutes max (target 2:45)

Record with screen capture (OBS / Windows Game Bar Win+G) over the live Cloud Run URL. Speak naturally; timings are guides.

**[0:00–0:20] The hook (talking over title/README):**
"₹1,729 crore of MPLADS constituency funds are lying unspent in India — not because money or citizen need is missing, but because deciding *which* works to sanction is hard. Existing tools collect complaints or track works after sanction. JanSetu AI is the missing decision layer."

**[0:20–0:50] Citizen side — photo report:**
Open Report tab. Upload a pothole/garbage photo. "A citizen just takes a photo — no typing, no literacy needed. Gemini Vision classifies the category, department and severity, and files a structured grievance. This works offline too — reports queue on the phone and sync when signal returns."

**[0:50–1:20] Voice + multilingual chat:**
Switch language to हिन्दी, tap 🎙️, ask in Hindi ("पिछले महीने सबसे ज़्यादा शिकायतें किस वार्ड से आईं?"). "Ask in any of 12 Indian languages by voice. Gemini converts the question to SQL, runs it on constituency data, answers in the same language — and reads it aloud."

**[1:20–1:45] Dashboard + map:**
Open Dashboard. "The office sees spike alerts — streetlight complaints in Kisan Basti running 27× baseline — and a compliance radar: SC/ST spend is 15.9% against the mandatory 22.5%, with the corrective amount computed: ₹106 lakh." Open Hotspots map. "The same wards glow on the demand map."

**[1:45–2:25] The core — Priority Works briefing:**
Open Priority Works, click Generate. "This is the heart of JanSetu. Gemini fuses citizen demand, spike detection, ward demographics and the live MPLADS fund ledger into ranked, costed, compliant sanctionable works. Notice the SC/ST-mandate badges." Click "Draft recommendation letter". "One click — a formal letter to the District Magistrate, ready to sign. From citizen voice to sanctioned work."

**[2:25–2:45] Close:**
"Built on five Google services — Gemini, BigQuery, Cloud Translation, Cloud Run, Google Maps — with a three-tier fallback chain, so it never breaks even if API quotas run out. One constituency is 25 lakh citizens; there are 543. JanSetu AI: the decisions just got easier."

**Tips:** rehearse once; keep browser zoom at 110–125%; close other tabs; if Gemini quota trips mid-recording, the fallback notice appearing is a FEATURE — narrate it ("even now, the platform degrades gracefully and keeps working").
